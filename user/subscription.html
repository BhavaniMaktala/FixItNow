Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to the new features. When downgrading, the changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is there a contract or long-term commitment?
No, all our plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can cancel your subscription at any time without any cancellation fees.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, UPI payments, and net banking. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.
Do you offer discounts for annual payments?
Yes! We offer a 15% discount when you pay annually for the Growth plan and a 20% discount for the Enterprise plan. Contact our sales team for custom enterprise pricing for larger teams.
